Congratulations!

You have passed the final example!

The final step is to receive a signed certificate from a California DMV approved instructor. This is an official certificate proving that you have completed the classroom phase of obtaining your drivers license. You can present this certificate to the DMV to obtain your permit.

Q. What happens next?

A. Once your order is completed, an instructor will review your final exam, sign the certificate and mail it to the address you provide.

How long does the review process normally take?

In most cases, the instructor will review, sign, and send the certificate to you within 48 hours.

How much does the instructor review, and delivery of signed DMV certificate cost?

The normal fee including shipping costs: $49.95

How long will it take for the certificate to reach me?

We normally use the United States Postal Service to send the certificate, this may take up to 7 working days.

Can I expedite the process?

Yes, we can send it via Federal Express Overnight Shipping (If you order before 3 P.M.) There will be an additional fee ($19.95) for expidited service.

What do I do after I get the certificate?

You should make an appointment at a California DMV office to obtain your learner's permit. Once you obtain the permit you must obtain another certificate showing that you have completed the practical portion. The practical portion typically consists of a 20 hour road course, on the road, in a car with a California approved DMV instructor. Once you have completed that portion, you can take the California DMV Road Exam to obtain your drivers license.

Do you provide the practical portion?

Caldrivers.com does not, we only provide the written exam. However, you can request one of our affiliates to contact you. You can then schedule the next part of your training with them.
